INGREDIENT

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Water
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Pepper
  • Maggi
  • Salt

Steps in preparing the dish

  1. Pour water inside the pot.
  2. Make sure you have washed the Rice and Beans with a clean water.
  3. After that first pour the beans inside the pot and leave it for about 15 minutes.
  4. Then pour the rice inside the pot too without puting salt inside it.
  5. Leave it for about 20 to 25 minutes on fire.
  6. After the rice and the beans are soft enough, then you can now put in the seasonings(maggi, salt,Groundnut oil and the pepper).
  7. And then give it another 5 minutes for everything to blend together.
  8. Enjoy you meal.
